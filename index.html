I am currently a Post Doctoral Fellow at the Victoria University of Wellington in New Zealand. At the moment I am on leave from my position as a Van Vleck Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, where I intend to return in August 2017.

My research is mostly in computability theory, a subfield of mathematical logic. I am mainly interested in algorithmic randomness and in the Medvedev and Muchnik lattices, but am also interested in varying other topics in or touching on computability theory in general.
